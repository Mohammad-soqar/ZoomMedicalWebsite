What is Hair Transplanting?

Hair transplanting is a surgical procedure that addresses hair loss by transplanting hair follicles from denser areas of the scalp, or other body parts, to the balding or thinning regions. This treatment is particularly effective for male pattern baldness and can also be employed to restore eyelashes, eyebrows, beard hair, chest hair, and to fill in scars caused by accidents or surgeries. The most common techniques for hair transplanting are Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) and Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE). Each method involves harvesting healthy hair follicles and grafting them onto the target areas, with the goal of achieving a natural-looking hair density and hairline.
